Mega Event Rules

Anveshnam as the word suggests mean "to investigate" Anveshnam is an event which includes clues based on a given theme. This is a team event. The entire team gets involved. The participants need team work perseverance and ofcourse to do !! lot of rush !!

Anveshnam

No. of Participants: whole team
The event consists of eleven Levels. If the team is found doing any kind of misconduct it will be disqualified. The team reaching the final spot of the puzzle will be considered as the winner.
