ABOUT ASHWAMEDHA

Ashwamedha is an initiation to spread the message of peace and happiness all around us. Ashwamedha conceived out of the inspiration of the legendary
Pt. Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Ji who believed in progress and 'Ekatm Manavdarshan' . We are highly privileged to conduct this fest for the second time which comprises of events based on literature, culture, sports and computers. It aims to promote the world of schooling by 'Reviving The Era of Rama' and moving with a thought process to be better than ever to make this world a better place to live in.
