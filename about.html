Our School

This institution is fortunate enough to be blessed by the eminent thinker Shri Madhav Rao Sadashiv Rao Golwalkar Guruji, the profound scholar Barrister Narendrajeet Singh Ji and the epitome of compassion Smt. Sushila Narendrajeet Singh Ji (Booji). The foundation of the institution was laid on 23rd February 1970, on the land given by Sri Brahmavarta Sanatan Dharam Mahamandal.

Pt. Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Sanatan Dharam Vidyalaya is a unique institution where education is imparted as a sacrament of Indian culture and values. We, at our Vidyalaya believe in the ancient traditions of our great culture and strive to bring the all round development of our students. Our Vidyalaya was established in the fond memory of the great visionary Pandit Deen Dayal Upadhyaya who believed that our ancient traditions should not only be observed but they should be adapted to the needs and requirements of the present generation.

We are spread over a 10acre campus. The massive structure comprises of various classrooms, hostels, library, laboratories, prayer hall known as ‘MARUTI MANDIR’, a multifunctional hall named ‘Deendayal Assembly Hall’ and a sports ground called ‘Madhav Smriti’. The prayer hall has a huge copper (Ashtadhatu) statue of Lord (Hanuman). Lush green lawns with beautiful flowers adorn the vast school campus giving aesthetic relief to the residents. It is a comfortable, and secure campus ideal for educational activities.

From The Principal Desk

School education lays the foundation for the future of the students and ensures a stable, successful and satisfied life. Education is a complete process that leads to the attainment of the full potential of the child. Our endeavor is to equip our students with life-skills to face the real world and facilitate them to be global citizens with tolerance, respect and appreciation of diverse cultures and religions for a life-time learning experience. We believe in inculcating strong values by converting every individual into a self-reliant and independent citizen for which the school provides an amalgam of scholastic and co-scholastic activities.
-Ms. Rekha Pachauri
