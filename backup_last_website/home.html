About School

Pt. Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Sanatan Dharma Vidyalaya was established with the aim to educate the students with sacrament of Indian culture and values, with continuous improvement in our system, facilities and amenities. Our value driven education is expressed through core values which are woven into every academic and co-curricular activity.In today era, it is essential that children inculcate sense of responsibility, integrity, courtesy, respect, cultural understanding, social sensitivity and ethical behaviour through the traditional values and cultures of our country.

About Ashwamedha

Ashwamedha is an initiation to spread the message of peace and happiness all around us. Ashwamedha conceived out of the inspiration of the legendary Pt. Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Ji who believed in progress and 'Ekatm Manavdarshan' . We are highly privileged to conduct this fest for the second time which comprises of events based on literature, culture, sports and computers. It aims to promote the world of schooling by 'Reviving The Era of Rama' and moving with a thought process to be better than ever to make this world a better place to live in.
